# Email Notification Setup - Quick Summary

## ✅ What You'll Receive

When a customer books a photoshoot through your website, you will receive an email at **srichaithanyacseaiml@gmail.com** with:

### Customer Details:
- ✅ Customer Name
- ✅ Customer Email Address
- ✅ Customer Phone Number

### Booking Details:
- ✅ Service Type (Portrait, Wedding, Event, Nature, or Other)
- ✅ Preferred Date (formatted nicely)
- ✅ Booking Time (when the booking was submitted)

### Customer Message:
- ✅ The message/notes from the customer

---

## 🚀 Quick Setup (5 Minutes)

1. **Sign up at EmailJS** (free): https://www.emailjs.com/
2. **Connect your Gmail** (srichaithanyacseaiml@gmail.com)
3. **Create email template** (copy from SETUP_INSTRUCTIONS.md)
4. **Update script.js** with your 3 credentials:
   - Public Key
   - Service ID
   - Template ID

That's it! Once configured, every booking will automatically send you an email.
